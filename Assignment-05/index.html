# Chicken and Dumplings # Ingredients
  • 3 celery ribs, chopped
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced
  • 3 cans (14-1/2 ounces each) reduced-sodium chicken broth
  • 3 cups cubed cooked chicken breast
  • 1/3 teaspoon poultry seasoning.

  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1-2/3 cups reduced-fat biscuit/baking mix
  • 2/3 cup fat-free milk
# Directions
  1. In a Dutch oven coated with cooking spray, cook and stir celery and carrots over medium heat until tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in broth, chicken and seasonings.
  3. Bring to a boil.

  4. reduce heat to a gentle simmer.

  5. For dumplings, mix biscuit mix and milk until a soft dough forms.
  6. Drop by tablespoonfuls on top of the simmering liquid.
  7. Reduce heat to low; cover and cook until a toothpick inserted in dumplings comes out clean (do not lift cover during the first 10 minutes)
  8. Cool and serve!
